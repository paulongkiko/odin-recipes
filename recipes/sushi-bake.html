Sushi Bake

Description

Sushi bake is essentailly a deconstructed California sushi roll. The ingredients are layered and baked like a casserole. When served, the dish is scooped onto sheets of dried seaweed before consuming.

Ingedients

  • Sushi Rice (Koshikari)
  • Crab / Imitation Crab
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Cream Cheese
  • Furikake

Steps

  1. Make your sushi rice. You can use plain cooked rice, but consider taking the time to add vinegar, sugar, and salt to enhance the flavor or your bake.
  2. Spread the sushi rice into an oven safe dish and sprinkle with furikake.
  3. In a bowl, mix shredded crab with cream cheese and kewpie mayo, and proceed to spread it on top of the rice layer in your dish. Then sprinkle on more furikake.
  4. Put the sushi bake into the oven and bake until it starts to brown, bubble, and is heated through.
  5. Remove from the oven, and, if desired, drizzle with more kewpie mayo and sriracha. Scoop onto roasted seaweed and enjoy.
